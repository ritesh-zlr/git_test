Odia style mutton masala

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Mutton
  • 1/2 kg Onion
  • as per taste Salt
  • 3 tbsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 2 Green chilli
  • 7 tbsp Mustard oil
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1 inch Cinnamon
  • 3 Green cardamom
  • 1 Black cardamom
  • 4 Cloves
  • 5 Black pepper
  • 1 tblsp Cumin powder
  • 2 tblsp Coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp meat masala
  • 1 tbsp garam masala

Cooking Method

  1. Wash mutton and remove all excess fat.
  2. Then cut into bite size pieces.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or deep skillet.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste and saut√© for 1 minure.
  5. Now add mutton and cook for 10 minutes or until its color changes to a golden brown.
  6. Put yogurt in it and cook for 5 minutes or until all water dries up.
  7. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes then add 2 glass water.
  9. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to low.
  10. Cover with a lid and let it simmer for 40 minutes or until meat is tender.
  11. After 40 minutes, remove the lid.
  12. Cook on high heat for 5-8 minutes or until all water dries up.
  13. Sprinkle hot spices powder over it.
  14. Take it out in a bowl.
  15. Garnish with coriander leaves, mint leaves and sliced ginger
  16. Mutton Masala is ready to serve.
